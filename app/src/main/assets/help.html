Weather App 9000

Welcome user! You can use this app to keep track of the weather at your favourite locations.

Wait, but how?

In these 3 simple steps:

  1. On the home screen, tap the floating plus button
  2. Select a location from the map and confirm your selection.
  3. Receive weather information!

The magic doesn't end here! Next time you use the app, this location will be easily accessible from the home screen

To remove a favourite location, simply tap the trash can icon next to it's name, voila!

Enjoy!
